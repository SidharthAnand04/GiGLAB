Did you know that according to a recent study, only 32% of traditional students feel prepared for their careers?

How about that only 20% of employers believe students are prepared for career management?

These statistics may not be all that surprising based on your own experiences. Most of our formal education focuses on gaining competency in a specific domain such as computer science, business, engineering, or design. If your experience is like most, however, very little time was dedicated to teaching you the skills that would help you become and remain employable throughout your career. Furthermore, after you’ve entered the labor market, career development investment made by your employer is typically more directed towards the needs of that employer as opposed to your own personal career development goals.

Mission

GiGLAB, was founded to curate resources and create strategic frameworks with the sole purpose of helping you manage your career. We make you our priority and go to work developing tools and resources to help you meet your career goals. And, as the nature of work, the organization of the global labor market, and the needs of the economy inevitably change, we refine and adapt the tools and resources we provide to these changing needs.

We do the work for you, so you are always prepared.
